BIO

BLAKE “BLIZZY” ROBINSON IS A WORLD PROFOUND SELF TAUGHT DRUMMER WHO TOURS THE NATION WITH RAPPER, SONGWRITER AND PRODUCER G-EAZY. BLIZZY REGULARLY ATTRACTS MEDIA EXPOSURE HERE IN THE U.S. AND ABROAD DUE TO HIS RAPID GROWTH IN THE MUSIC INDUSTRY. HE HAS PERFORMED ON TALK SHOWS SUCH AS, JIMMY KIMMEL LIVE, LATE NIGHT WITH SETH MEYERS AND JIMMY FALLON JUST TO NAME A FEW. HE CONTINUES TO TAKE THE INDUSTRY BY STORM WITH THE LATEST FASHION AND WAS FEATURED IN XXL MAGAZINE AS ONE OF THE “12 MOST STYLIST PEOPLE AT AGENDA NYC” 2015. WITH A LARGE SOCIAL MEDIA FOLLOWING, HE’S ABLE TO MAINTAIN AND ATTRACT NEW FOLLOWERS DUE TO HIS CLOTHING LINE (THE LOYALTY CLUB) IN ADDITION TO HIS RELATIONSHIP WITH INFLUENTIAL ARTISTS IN THE MUSIC INDUSTRY. BLIZZY'S PASSION FOR DRUMMING BEGAN IN HIS FATHERS’ CHURCH WHERE HE ROUTINELY RETURNS AND HOLDS LESSONS FOR THE YOUNGER GENERATION COMING UP BEHIND HIM...
